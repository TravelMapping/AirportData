About TM Airports

Welcome to TM Airports, a member-driven project designed to track, map, and share your lifetime commercial flight history. Inspired by the framework of the original Travel Mapping highway-tracking project, this site shifts the focus from highways to runways, allowing aviation enthusiasts to visualize their travels globally.

How it works: The site operates on a simple, automated pipeline. Members submit a plain-text .alist file containing their airport data. Every day, an automated backend processes these files to dynamically update our global flight statistics, traveler leaderboards, and interactive airport maps.
